Tarot is a form of fortunetelling, using a standardized set of cards, listed here. Click on a card to learn more about it's meaning.

Tarot Cards are typically drawn at random, in a spread of a given number of cards. If a card is placed upside down (called being 'reversed'), this is considered in the fortune told, with an upside down card representing a negative aspect of that which the card represents.
